Classic Chocolate Chip Cookies: Preheat oven to 375°F. Mix 1 cup softened butter with 3/4 cup sugar and 3/4 cup brown sugar until creamy. Beat in 2 eggs and 2 teaspoons vanilla extract. Combine 2 1/4 cups flour, 1 teaspoon baking soda, and 1 teaspoon salt, then gradually blend into butter mixture. Stir in 2 cups chocolate chips. Drop rounded tablespoons onto ungreased cookie sheets. Bake for 9-11 minutes until golden brown. Cool and enjoy!
