The old lighthouse stood alone on the rocky cliff, its beam cutting through the thick fog like a guardian watching over the restless sea. For decades, keeper Thomas had climbed those spiral stairs each evening, faithful to his duty. Tonight felt different. As he reached the top, he noticed a bottle wedged between the stones, containing a faded map and a single word scrawled in ancient ink: "Remember." His heart raced as memories of a forgotten adventure flooded back.
